Legal Compliance

Compliance with legal regulations is paramount to us. We meticulously research and adhere to all relevant local, state, and national regulations governing the sale and adoption of cats. By doing so, we ensure that our practices meet the highest legal standards.

Promoting Adoption

While we provide a platform for cat sales, we also advocate for adoption whenever possible. We actively partner with local animal shelters and rescue organizations to facilitate adoptions, promoting responsible pet ownership and supporting efforts to reduce animal homelessness and overpopulation.

Health and Well-being

The health and well-being of our cats are of utmost importance to us. Before being offered for adoption or sale, each cat receives proper veterinary care, vaccinations, and health screenings. We transparently provide potential adopters or buyers with detailed information about the cat's health history and medical treatments.

Thorough Screening Process

We implement a thorough screening process for potential adopters or buyers to ensure they can provide a safe and loving home for their new companion. Our process may include adoption applications, home visits, and references from veterinarians or previous pet owners.

Education and Support

Our commitment to our customers doesn't end after the adoption process. We offer comprehensive educational resources and ongoing support to new cat owners, empowering them to provide the best care possible for their furry friends. At Furr Sale, we believe that responsible pet ownership starts with a compassionate and ethical approach. Join us in creating a positive impact on the lives of cats and their new owners.
